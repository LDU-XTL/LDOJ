</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p>Farmer John has taken his cows on a trip to the city! As the sun sets, the cows gaze at the city horizon and observe the beautiful silhouettes formed by the rectangular buildings.</p><p>The entire horizon is represented by a number line with <i>N</i> (1 &le; <i>N</i> &le; 40,000) buildings. Building <i>i</i>'s silhouette has a base that spans locations <i>A<sub>i</sub></i> through <i>B<sub>i</sub></i> along the horizon (1 &le; <i>A<sub>i</sub></i> &lt; <i>B<sub>i</sub></i> &le; 1,000,000,000) and has height <i>H<sub>i</sub></i> (1 &le; <i>H<sub>i</sub></i> &le; 1,000,000,000). Determine the area, in square units, of the aggregate silhouette formed by all <i>N</i> buildings.</p></div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">Line 1: A single integer: <i>N</i>
<br>Lines 2..<i>N</i>+1: Input line <i>i</i>+1 describes building <i>i</i> with three space-separated integers: <i>A<sub>i</sub></i>, <i>B<sub>i</sub></i>, and <i>H<sub>i</sub></i></div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">Line 1: The total area, in square units, of the silhouettes formed by all <i>N</i> buildings</div><p class="pst">Sample Input</p><pre class="sio">4
2 5 1
9 10 4
6 8 2
4 6 3</pre><p class="pst">Sample Output</p><pre class="sio">16</pre><p class="pst">Hint</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">The first building overlaps with the fourth building for an area of 1 square unit, so the total area is just 3*1 + 1*4 + 2*2 + 2*3 - 1 = 16.</div><p class="pst">
