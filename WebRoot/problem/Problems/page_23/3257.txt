</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p>The cows are building a roller coaster! Each component <i>i</i> has a fixed length <i>W<sub>i</sub></i> (1 &le; <i>W<sub>i</sub></i> &le; <i>L</i>). Help the cows determine the most fun roller coaster that they can build with their budget.</p></div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">Line 1: Three space-separated integers: <i>L</i>, <i>N</i> and <i>B</i>.
