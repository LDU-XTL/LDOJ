The next N lines contain M integers each. The integers are separated by one or more spaces.</p></div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p>This unique line contains the sum of the numbers on the found evolvent.</p></div><p class="pst">Sample Input</p><pre class="sio">4 4
