</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p>An image <i>A</i> is said to be a subimage of another image <i>B</i> if it is possible to remove some rows and/or columns of pixels from <i>B</i> so that the resulting image is identical to <i>A</i>. Figure 6 illustrates an example. Image <i>A</i>, shown in Figure 6(a), is a subimage of image <i>B</i>, shown in Figure 6(b), because the image resulting from the removal of the middle row and column of pixels from <i>B</i> is identical to <i>A</i>.</p><div align="center"><table><tr><td align="center"><table style=" border-collapse: collapse;"><tr><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;" bgcolor="black">&nbsp;</td><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;" bgcolor="black">&nbsp;</td></tr><tr><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;" bgcolor="black">&nbsp;</td><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;">&nbsp;</td></tr></table></td><td style="width: 36pt"></td><td align="center"><table style="border-collapse: collapse"><tr><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;" bgcolor="black">&nbsp;</td><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;">&nbsp;</td><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;" bgcolor="black">&nbsp;</td></tr><tr><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;">&nbsp;</td><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;">&nbsp;</td><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;">&nbsp;</td></tr><tr><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;" bgcolor="black">&nbsp;</td><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;">&nbsp;</td><td style="width: 1em; height: 1em; border: solid black 1pt;">&nbsp;</td></tr></table></td></tr><tr style="font-size: 10pt"><td align="center">(a) Image <i>A</i></td><td style="width: 36pt"></td><td align="center">(b) Image <i>B</i></td></tr></table></div><p align="center">Figure 6: An example of a subimage</p><p>Given two black-and-white images <i>A</i> and <i>B</i>, determine whether <i>A</i> is a subimage of <i>B</i>.</p></div><p class="pst">Input</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US"><p>The input contains a single test case.
