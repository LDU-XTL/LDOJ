There are at most 100 people at the picnic. </div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">Your output will be a single line containing 2 numbers: the total weight of the people on one team, and the total weight of the people on the other team. </pre><p class="pst">Sample Output</p><pre class="sio">190 200
