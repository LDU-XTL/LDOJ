</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">There is an old stone game.At the beginning of the game the player picks n(1<=n<=50000) piles of stones in a line. The goal is to merge the stones in one pile observing the following rules:
<br>At each step of the game,the player can merge two adjoining piles to a new pile.The score is the number of stones in the new pile.
<br>You are to write a program to determine the minimum of the total score.

<br>The last test case is followed by one zero.
<br></div><p class="pst">Output</p><div class="ptx" lang="en-US">For each test case output the answer on a single line.You may assume the answer will not exceed 1000000000.</div><p class="pst">Sample Input</p><pre class="sio">1
